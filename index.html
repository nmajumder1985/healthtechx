Plain language translation
Dr. Sarah Chen has prescribed Metformin 500 mg, to be taken by mouth twice a day, for patient Ahmed Al-Rashid. This is an active order with a 90-day supply and 3 refills permitted. The prescription was issued to treat Type 2 diabetes mellitus and is coded against internationally recognised clinical terminologies so any connected system — pharmacy, EHR, or payer — can read and act on it without manual re-entry.
